Images

Aspect-ratio and object-fit utilities for images and media content.

Examples (anchor)

The aspect-ratio and object-fit utilities can be applied to a single image or a container to apply the styles to child elements. Both options including fixed classes with predefined values and variable classes for customizing inline.

Single images (anchor)

Fixed

Example HTML 
<img src="/examples/img/elephant-480.webp" class="object-cover ratio-3x4" alt="Head on shot of elephant with big tusks and ears flared staring down camera" width="180">
<img src="/examples/img/elephant-480.webp" class="object-cover ratio-1x1" alt="Head on shot of elephant with big tusks and ears flared staring down camera" width="180">
<img src="/examples/img/elephant-480.webp" class="object-cover ratio-16x9" alt="Head on shot of elephant with big tusks and ears flared staring down camera" width="180">
<img src="/examples/img/elephant-480.webp" class="object-cover ratio-5x2" alt="Head on shot of elephant with big tusks and ears flared staring down camera" width="180">
<img src="/examples/img/elephant-480.webp" class="object-cover ratio-4x1" alt="Head on shot of elephant with big tusks and ears flared staring down camera" width="180">

Variable

Example HTML 
<img src="/examples/img/desert-480.webp" alt="Desert sand dunes with blue sky background" width="300" class="object-cover ratio" style="--ratio: 15 / 7;">
<img src="/examples/img/desert-480.webp" alt="Desert sand dunes with blue sky background" width="300" class="object-cover ratio" style="--ratio: 15 / 6;">
<img src="/examples/img/desert-480.webp" alt="Desert sand dunes with blue sky background" width="300" class="object-cover ratio" style="--ratio: 15 / 5;">

Child images (anchor)

Fixed

Example HTML 
<div class="child-object-cover child-ratio-1x1 grid g-2-xs g-4 gap-1 mb-3">
<img src="/examples/img/rocky-stream-480.webp" alt="A rocky stream next to a small copse of pinetrees on a grassy bank" width="300">
<img src="/examples/img/red-fox-480.webp" alt="A red fox with one eye blue and the other brown looking directly at camera" width="300">
<img src="/examples/img/hazy-mountains-480.webp" alt="View of distant mountains and valleys scenery bathed in hazy sunlight" width="300">
<img src="/examples/img/bird-on-branch-480.webp" alt="A small white and brown bird with orange chest colors sitting on a tree branch" width="300">
</div>

Variable

Example HTML 
<div class="child-ratio child-object-cover grid g-2-xs g-4 gap-1 mb-3" style="--ratio: 15 / 7">
<img src="/examples/img/elephant-480.webp" alt="Head on shot of elephant with big tusks and ears flared staring down camera" width="300">
<img src="/examples/img/frog-540.webp" alt="Close up of green frog sitting on leaf of tree." width="300">
<img src="/examples/img/lion-540.webp" alt="Male lion with healthy main staring into distance." width="300">
<img src="/examples/img/red-fox-480.webp" alt="A red fox with one eye blue and the other brown looking directly at camera" width="300">
</div>

</figure>
